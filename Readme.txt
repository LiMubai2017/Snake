1.Description
   This is a little game,which has four different modes for playing;
   Level 1 and Level 2 are both for a single player while the Level3 and Level4 are for two players;
2.How to play
   In the level1 and Level2,use↑↓←→  to control the snake to make it grow longer and you will get more grades.
   In the level3 and Level4,the player in the left shall use wasd to control,while the one in the right use↑↓←→.
   The blank is used to pause in all levels.
